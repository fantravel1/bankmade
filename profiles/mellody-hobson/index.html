Mellody Hobson

Co-CEO, Ariel Investments

Age 55 • United States • Chicago, Illinois

A champion of disciplined value investing and financial literacy. Mellody Hobson co-leads Ariel Investments, one of the largest African-American-owned asset managers, while championing financial education for underserved communities. Chair of Starbucks board.

$15.5B+ Assets Under Management
30+ Years in Investing
500K+ Families Reached (Financial Literacy)
2004 Co-CEO at Ariel

Patient Capital, Boundless Vision

Mellody Hobson represents a different archetype of female financial leader than many of her peers. While Cathie Wood commands media attention and Mary Meeker publishes trends reports, Hobson operates with quiet discipline, building institutional credibility through decades of superior returns and unwavering commitment to value investing.

But what distinguishes Hobson even further is her commitment to financial literacy and economic empowerment. She recognized that controlling capital is insufficient if that capital doesn't translate into shared prosperity. Her work educating families, particularly in underserved communities, about the importance of investing in diversified portfolios has impacted hundreds of thousands of lives.

As Co-CEO of Ariel Investments—one of the largest African-American-owned asset management firms in America—Hobson has demonstrated that excellence in capital management and commitment to diversity and inclusion are not competing objectives but mutually reinforcing ones.

📅 Career Arc 📅

Building Ariel & Financial Freedom

1991

Joins Ariel Investments

Mellody Hobson joins Ariel Investments as an entry-level investment analyst. She brings intelligence, work ethic, and a commitment to understanding her clients' long-term needs.

2000

Vice President & Portfolio Manager

Ascends to Vice President and becomes a portfolio manager. Her disciplined value investing approach delivers consistent returns in various market environments, earning trust of both colleagues and clients.

2004

Co-CEO, Ariel Investments

Becomes Co-CEO of Ariel Investments, sharing leadership with founder John Rogers. Together they expand the firm from approximately $3 billion to over $15 billion in assets under management.

2008

Financial Literacy Advocacy

Founds and leads Ariel's financial education initiatives, creating programs to teach families about investing, diversification, and long-term wealth building. The programs reach hundreds of thousands.

2015

Chair of Starbucks Board

Becomes Chair of the Starbucks Board of Directors, one of the most visible female board leadership roles in corporate America. Brings institutional investment experience to corporate governance.

2020-2025

Continued Leadership & Expansion

Continues expanding Ariel's investment reach while intensifying financial literacy work. Recognized as a leading advocate for diversity and inclusion in the investment industry.

Value Investing Philosophy & Approach

Long-Term Conviction

Hobson invests with 10+ year horizons, allowing her portfolios to compound through multiple market cycles. She resists the temptation to trade frequently or chase short-term trends.

Fundamental Research

Deep company and industry analysis drives investment decisions. Hobson's teams conduct thorough due diligence before committing capital, understanding competitive dynamics, management quality, and valuation carefully.

Risk-Adjusted Returns

Rather than maximizing returns at any cost, Hobson focuses on delivering attractive returns while managing risk. This philosophy appeals to institutional clients prioritizing capital preservation alongside growth.

Diversity & Inclusion

Hobson has made diversifying the investment industry a priority—both in hiring talented professionals from underrepresented backgrounds and in educating diverse communities about investing opportunities.

Impact & Legacy

Building African-American Wealth

Ariel Investments under Hobson's co-leadership has grown into a beacon of success for African-American ownership in finance. The firm demonstrates that talented investors of color can deliver institutional-quality returns while serving diverse client bases.

Financial Literacy Champion

Through Ariel's financial literacy programs, Hobson has directly impacted hundreds of thousands of families. She has democratized investment knowledge, teaching families that long-term investing in diversified portfolios is accessible to everyone, not just the wealthy.

Corporate Board Leadership

As Chair of Starbucks board, Hobson has elevated the visibility of female leadership in corporate governance. Her presence signals that women belong in the highest levels of corporate decision-making and capital allocation.
